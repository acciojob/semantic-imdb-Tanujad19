Movie Summary

Gangs of Wasseypur - Part 1is an Indian crime drama directed by Anurag Kashyap. The story follows the power struggles, vengeance, and politics among the coal mafia of Dhanbad, spanning several generations. It portrays the rise of Sardar Khan as he seeks revenge against Ramadhir Singh for betraying his father.

Main Actors

  • Manoj Bajpayee as Sardar Khan
  • Nawazuddin Siddiqui as Faizal Khan
  • Tigmanshu Dhulia as Ramadhir Singh
  • Richa Chadha as Nagma Khatoon
  • Pankaj Tripathi as Sultan Qureshi
