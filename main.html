Gangs of Wasseypur - Part 1

Actors Quotes
Gangs of Wasseypur - Part 1 is a gritty Indian crime saga directed by Anurag Kashyap. It portrays the coal mafia of Dhanbad and Sardar Khan’s thirst for revenge against Ramadhir Singh, spanning across generations of rivalry, power, and politics.

Main Actors

  • Manoj Bajpayee as Sardar Khan
  • Nawazuddin Siddiqui as Faizal Khan
  • Tigmanshu Dhulia as Ramadhir Singh
  • Richa Chadha as Nagma Khatoon
  • Pankaj Tripathi as Sultan Qureshi
